## FULL DESCRIPTION

### What is Radar Narcisista?

Radar Narcisista is a web-based SaaS platform that helps people gain clarity in potentially abusive or toxic relationships. Using AI-powered journaling and pattern detection, it identifies manipulation, gaslighting, and emotional abuse patterns.

### The Problem It Solves

Millions of people live in relationships where they suffer emotional abuse but can't see the pattern. Radar provides:

- **Guided journaling** to record episodes
- **Automatic risk detection** via AI
- **Visual indicators** of problematic behaviors
- **AI Coach (Oracle)** for reflection
- **Professional dashboard** for therapists

### Tech Stack
